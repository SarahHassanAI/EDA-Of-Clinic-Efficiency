# Clinic Operational Efficiency: A Data-Driven EDA to Slash Wait Times & Boost Patient Satisfaction
## Executive Summary:
Patient satisfaction scores were declining at a Dubai clinic due to **long wait times**, risking patient churn and revenue. Using Python and Power BI, I identified key bottlenecks in patient flow and developed a business action plan. The analysis reveals opportunities to **reduce average wait times from 22.1 to 15 minutes** and **increase patient satisfaction from 3.7 to 4.3 stars**, potentially protecting **AED 85,000+ in annual revenue**.

## The Business Problem:

Patient satisfaction is directly tied to clinic revenue and retention in **Dubai's competitive healthcare market**. Clinic stakeholders noticed satisfaction scores were declining (averaging 3.74/5) with 38.9% of patients experiencing long waits over 25 minutes. How can we determine where bottlenecks are occurring in the patient journey and make adjustments to improve patient satisfaction?

## Methodology 
Leveraging my background in clinical and insurance operations, I:

1. Developed a **Python ETL pipeline** to automate data cleaning & feature engineering

2. Created a **Power BI dashboard** to provide real-time visibility into clinic operations and KPI tracking

3. Performed statistical **modeling and bottleneck** **analysis** to identify pain points
   
_Note: For this project scope, a CSV file was used. In a production environment, a direct database connection (e.g., SQL) would be the scalable best practice._
